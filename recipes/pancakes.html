Pancakes

Description

Nothing beats waking up in the morn' and thinking, "boy, I'm very hungry." And then you head downstairs and suddenly, there they are. A classic breakfast goodie covered in syrup and berries, and the smell...oh, wow, the smell. Aint' nothiing like a good ol' stack of pancakes.

Everyone wants to know how to makes this classic breakfast. Unfortunately, the best we can hope for is...ugh, boxed powder. No, thank you. I'd rather make my own using my favorite recipe below.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 beaten egg

Steps:

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in large bowl. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, oil and egg. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!
