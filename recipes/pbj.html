Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

You wanna know my favorite thing from my childhood? A simple yet delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich. It's an experience shared by many people throughout time and space. If you have never had the pleasure of indulging in one of these heavenly marvels, then you're gonna love this recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly or Jam
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps:

  1. Lay one slice of bread on the table and spread peanut butter on it
  2. Lay the other slice down next to it and spread jelly on that slice
  3. With the contents of each slice facing each other, stack them together
  4. Enjoy!
